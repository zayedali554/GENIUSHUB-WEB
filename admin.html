Add new Notes and DPP entries to Google Sheets

How to Use This Form

  1. Copy and paste a comma-separated line in this format:
  2. Subject,Chapter,Section,Title,Link
  3. Example:
  4. Biology,Cell - The Unit of Life (Botany),Notes,Cell - The Unit of Life 08 : Class Notes || Uday 2026 (Class 11th),https://static.pw.live/5eb393ee95fab7468a79d189/ADMIN/5a5a5222-b017-469e-b108-fa8d36c15c1e.pdf
  5. Click "Add Entry to Google Sheets"

Note: This form requires Google Apps Script to be set up on your Google Sheets.
